SIPDA Scheme (Accessibility & Rehabilitation)

Focuses on rehabilitation and providing barrier-free access as per the RPwD Act, 2016.

  • Promotes universal accessibility (ramps, lifts, adapted toilets, accessible websites).
  • Supports skill development programs and the issuance of disability certificates.

National Scholarship Schemes

Various scholarship programs to encourage education for students with disabilities.

  • Includes Post-matric Scholarship, Top Class Education Scholarship, and National Overseas Scholarships.
